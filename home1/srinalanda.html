About Nalanda

This Schools takes pride in the holistic and innovative learning methods which have helped them raise the bar in the field of education. The centre follows a well-researched curriculum that is based on the play way method of learning. This school offers education for children in different age groups. There are a host of other supplementary activities that children can enroll in to enhance their skill set
